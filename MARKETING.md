# Marketing & Monetization Strategy: Crisis Communication & PR Response Generator

## Market Opportunity

### Market Size
- **PR/crisis management market:** $14.8B globally (2024), growing 7.3% annually
- **SMB crisis vulnerability:** 75% of small businesses will face a reputational crisis within 5 years (Institute for Crisis Management)
- **Response time matters:** Companies that respond within 1 hour of a crisis have 3x better reputation recovery than those that wait 24+ hours
- **Target buyers:** 33M+ small businesses in US, 73M+ freelancers and consultants, 500K+ SaaS companies

### Problem Intensity
- Every business will have a crisis. A 1-star viral review, a data breach, a food safety issue, a bad customer experience that goes viral. Most have no plan.
- First-responder advantage: the first statement shapes the entire narrative. Without it, journalists, competitors, and social media write the story for you.
- Cost of professional help: PR crisis firms charge $5K-$25K retainer just to be on call. Crisis coaches: $300-$500/hr. Most SMBs can't access this — they're on their own.

## Target Customer Segments

### Primary: SMB Owner in Crisis
- **Size:** Any business 1-50 employees
- **Trigger:** Something bad just happened (bad review, incident, viral post)
- **Pain:** Doesn't know what to say, terrified of making it worse
- **Value prop:** Professional response in 30 minutes, not 30 hours
- **Price sensitivity:** Low — in a crisis, $29 is nothing

### Secondary: SaaS Founder
- **Trigger:** Data breach, major outage, security incident
- **Pain:** Has an engineer-heavy team with no PR expertise
- **Value prop:** Technical incident → professional public statement fast
- **Additional outputs needed:** Status page update, customer email sequence (pair with Skill #64)

### Tertiary: PR Professional / Agency
- **Use:** First draft generator for client crises
- **Value prop:** Cuts drafting time from 4 hours to 30 minutes
- **Margin:** Agency charges $500/hr, pays $29 for the skill — pure margin
